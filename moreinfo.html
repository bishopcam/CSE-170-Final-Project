More Info

This is a project to optimize the pedestrian crossings on the University of San Diego campus. The goal is to reduce the amount of time pedestrians spend waiting to cross the street. This is done by trying to find the best method for crossings, like pedestrian bridges, barriers in traditional crossings, tunnels, and more.
This website is so you can explore the types of crossings, through a simulation and some comparison tools, and decide on what you would like on your campus. Feel free to also use the Suggestions page to send an email to the creators of the website to suggest new methods for street crossings on campus.
